ITB is one of the best universities in Indonesia and Asia with a strong academic reputation and research atmosphere, with a number of top scientists who are globally recognized, in the fields of natural and human sciences, engineering, art and design, and business and management. By upholding the motto of being "Globally Respected, Locally Relevant", ITB organizes an international inbound semester exchange program that offers academic programs combined with a unique mix of cultures and geographies of Indonesia, particularly the city of Bandung. With the challenges faced, you are welcome to explore the future of technology and humanity with ITB.
List of Packages

Students can choose several courses (maximum 22 credits per semester for Bachelor Degree or maximum 12 credits per semester for Master Degree), maximum from two faculties.

Regular Program
If you are interested to take some courses in ITB, you can choose the regular program. The Regular program offers more than 100 courses which you can choose up to 22 ITB credit unit. You can combine the Package program with the courses of the regular program also.

Tuition Fees
The tuition waived policy of ITB Student-Exchange programs is based on the agreement between ITB and the partner institution or university. If you are not from one of the ITB partner institutions or universities with MoU/MoA regarding the student exchanges tuition waiver, please contact the International Office at your home institution or university.
The tuition fees for ITB Student-Exchange Program students from non-ITB partner institutions or universities are:
